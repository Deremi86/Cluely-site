Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cluely really undetectable?

Yes. Unlike other AI assistants, Cluely does not join the meeting as a bot. It runs as a private system layer on your computer, meaning it only captures audio from your speakers/mic. No one else on the call can see it.

Does it work with Zoom and Google Meet?

It works with every platform. Because Cluely processes your system audio, it is compatible with Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Slack, Discord, and even browser-based calls.

How fast are the AI responses?

Our optimized infrastructure delivers responses in as little as 300ms. This allows you to have a natural conversation without long, awkward pauses while waiting for the AI.
